# Dhune.np Help Center Knowledge

## About Dhune.np
Dhune.np is a multi-vendor laundry booking and management marketplace for Nepal. Customers create laundry requests, vendors submit offers, customers accept an offer, and the accepted offer becomes an order that can be tracked through pickup, processing, delivery, and completion.

## User Registration And Login
Customers can register or log in to use Dhune.np features. Public help can explain the general process, but account-specific profile, address, phone, email, OTP, and private login details must only be handled after login or through official support.

## Vendor Registration And Approval
Vendors can register and submit business details or documents when required. Admins review vendor information and approve eligible vendors before they can fully operate on the platform. The chatbot must not approve vendors or make approval decisions.

## Laundry Request Creation
Customers create a laundry request by choosing a service category, entering laundry details, pickup and delivery information, notes if needed, and submitting the request. Vendors can then review the request and send offers.

## Vendor Offer Process
Vendors respond to customer laundry requests with offers. Offers can include price, expected delivery or turnaround time, and service notes. Customers compare offers before accepting one.

## Offer Acceptance And Order Creation
When a customer accepts a vendor offer, the platform creates an order from that accepted offer. The chatbot can explain this flow but must not accept offers or create orders.

## Order Lifecycle
Orders can move through statuses such as pending or confirmed, pickup coordination, picked up, processing, ready for delivery, out for delivery, delivered, completed, cancelled, or disputed depending on the platform workflow. Customers should log in to view their own order status.

## Payment Methods
Dhune.np supports payment through Khalti, eSewa, and Cash on Delivery when enabled for an order. Khalti and eSewa are digital payment options. Cash on Delivery means the customer pays by cash according to the order flow. The chatbot must not process payments, verify payments, expose transaction IDs, or change payment status.

## Reviews And Ratings
Customers can submit reviews and ratings after eligible orders. Reviews help other users understand service quality and help admins monitor the marketplace. The chatbot must not create, edit, or delete reviews.

## Dispute Process
Customers can raise a dispute when there is a problem with an order, payment, delivery, or service. Admins review disputes and platform records. The public chatbot can explain how disputes work generally, but it must not open, modify, resolve, or expose dispute details.

## Notifications
Dhune.np may send notifications for important events such as request updates, offers, accepted offers, order status changes, payment updates, review prompts, and dispute updates. The chatbot must not read or expose private notifications.

## Categories And Services
Dhune.np service categories describe the types of laundry services available on the platform. Active public categories may be shown as general service information. Private vendor pricing, vendor financial records, and account-specific order details must not be exposed.

## Contact Support
For account-specific help, order-specific help, payment issues, vendor approval questions, document problems, disputes, or anything not answered by public help, users should log in to their Dhune.np account or contact Dhune.np support at support@dhune.np.

## Platform Rules
Customers should provide accurate pickup and delivery details, review offers carefully, and use official platform flows for payments and support. Vendors should provide clear offers, maintain service quality, and follow admin approval and platform rules. Admin-only operations are not available from the public chatbot.

## Chatbot Safety Rules
The chatbot is informational only. It must not create orders, process payments, approve vendors, cancel orders, update accounts, change disputes, update notifications, or modify database records except saving chatbot conversation history. It must not expose private user data, order details, payment details, transaction IDs, vendor financial records, phone numbers, emails, addresses, documents, OTPs, or admin-only information. If asked for private or account-specific information, tell the user to log in or contact Dhune.np support.
